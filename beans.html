How to Turn Our Beans Into a Perfect Cup

Brewing great coffee is a small ritual. Follow these simple steps and you will turn our beans into a cafe-quality cup at home.

1

Measure the Beans

Use about 18-20g beans for one rich cup and a balanced extraction.

2

Grind Fresh

Grind just before brewing; medium grind works for most methods.

3

Heat the Water

Target 90-96C for sweetness and clarity without harsh bitterness.

4

Brew Slowly

Pour steadily in stages and let the coffee bloom for 30 seconds.

5

Enjoy the Moment

Serve warm in your favorite mug and enjoy the aroma and texture.
